Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our remote internship

How long is the internship? ➕
The internship lasts 4–6 weeks depending on performance.
Is this internship remote? ➕
Yes, the internship is completely remote and flexible.
Are there any prerequisites? ➕
Basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript is expected.
Will I get a certificate? ➕
Yes, a certificate will be awarded upon successful completion.
How many hours per week are required? ➕
You should dedicate at least 10-12 hours per week.
Will there be mentorship or support? ➕
Yes, you’ll receive guidance and support through regular check-ins.
